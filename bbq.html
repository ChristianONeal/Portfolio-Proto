Old North State BBQ

This project was for a fictitious company called Old North State BBQ. Their thing is that they proudly serve both Eastern style BBQ AND Western style BBQ.The challenge in the logo was how to communicate both those things.

I wanted to show that the western style bbq was a tomato based sauce, hence the ketchup bottle, and the eastern style bbq was a vinegar based sauce, a la the vinegar bottle.

Here are several of my digital rough versions of the logo. It's starting to take shape, after creating the ketchup and vinegar bottles it was easy to create several options. Now just to find a lock-up that worked.

Here is the final version of the logo with a splash of color thrown in. It's subtle but the elements are there to suggest the multiple bbq options that are available at Old North State BBQ.
