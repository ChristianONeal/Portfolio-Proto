Cricket & Firefly

The client owns an interior design business in Cleveland Ohio and wanted a logo to represent her brand. She really wanted to tap into that feeling that she had growing up, in the summer enjoying warm evenings chasing fireflies outside with her family and friends.

Here are a few digital roughs to see if anything I had developed struck a chord with her. She was very excited about the stacked version of the logo and we pursued that direction.

We tried several different stacks until we landed on a final layout for Cricket and Firefly's new logo.

Here is the final version of the logo I created for Cricket and Firefly Interior Design Co based out of Cleveland, OH. The rounded corners and soft color palatte felt warm and friendly, and reflected the brands values very well.
